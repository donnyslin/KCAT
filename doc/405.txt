RALLYING - MAKINEN WINS 1,000 LAKES RALLY .
JYVASKYLA , Finland 1996-08-26
Tommi Makinen of Finland , driving a Mitsubishi , on Monday won the 1,000 Lakes Rally , sixth round of the world championship .
