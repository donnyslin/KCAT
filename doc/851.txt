Brazil gov't set to send 1997 budget to Congress .
BRASILIA 1996-08-29
Brazilian Planning Minister Antonio Kandir will submit to a draft copy of the 1997 federal budget to Congress on Thursday , a ministry spokeswoman said .
Congress is constitutionally obliged to approve the budget by the end of year but regularly fails to meet that requirement .
