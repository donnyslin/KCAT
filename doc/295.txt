PRESS DIGEST - Iraq - Aug 24 .
BAGHDAD 1996-08-24
These are some of the leading stories in the official Iraqi press on Saturday .
Reuters has not verified these stories and does not vouch for their accuracy .
JUMHOURIYA
- Istanbul chamber of commerce urges Ankara to resume trade with Iraq .
- Offers from Arab and foreign companies to supply Iraq with goods .
- Four ships unload tonnes of Iraq-bound sugar at Jordan 's Aqaba .
- Editorial blames U.S. for latest flare-up of fighting between Kurdish rebels in northern Iraq .
- Black market booms in the shadow of state-run supermarkets .
- Parliament completes draft law on protection of river waters in Iraq .
QADISSIYA
- Iraq denounces violation of airspace by U.S. warplanes .
IRAQ
- Editorial lambasts Jalal Talabani , leader of a Kurdish rebel faction in the north , for liaising with Iran in its fight against rivals .
BABEL
- Blaming Iraq for riots in Jordan is a dirty game .
