Israel 's Levy to meet Mubarak in Egypt .
JERUSALEM 1996-08-28
Israeli Foreign Minister David Levy will visit Egypt this Sunday for talks with President Hosni Mubarak , the Foreign Ministry said on Wednesday .
The trip will be Levy 's first to an Arab state as a minister in Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu 's government .
