Colombia police find marijuana on ship .
BOGOTA , Colombia 1996-08-29
Police said they found 35 metric tons of marijuana on Thursday on a ship preparing to set sail for the Netherlands from Colombia 's Caribbean port of Cartagena .
They said the drug had been packed into a shipping container and was surrounded by ground coffee .
No arrests had been made , a police spokesman said .
