SOCCER - HINCHCLIFFE CALLED INTO ENGLAND SQUAD .
LONDON 1996-08-25
England manager Glenn Hoddle called up uncapped Everton defender Andy Hinchcliffe on Sunday to the national squad for the opening World Cup qualifier against Moldova next weekend .
Left-back Hinchcliffe , 27 , replaces Tottenham 's Darren Anderton who has a recurring groin problem .
