CRICKET - AUSTRALIA BEAT ZIMBABWE BY 125 RUNS IN ONE-DAY MATCH .
COLOMBO 1996-08-26
Australia beat Zimbabwe by 125 runs in the first match of the Singer World Series one-day ( 50 overs ) cricket tournament on Monday .
Scores : Australia 263-7 in 50 overs , Zimbabwe 138 all out in 41 overs .
