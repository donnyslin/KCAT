SOCCER - KEANE SIGNS FOUR-YEAR CONTRACT WITH MANCHESTER UNITED .
LONDON 1996-08-22
Ireland midfielder Roy Keane has signed a new four-year contract with English league and F.A. Cup champions Manchester United .
" Roy agreed a new deal before last night 's game against Everton and we are delighted , " said United manager Alex Ferguson on Thursday .
